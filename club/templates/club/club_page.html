Hurtlocker

About

The Hurtlocker Training System is a unique approach to martial arts and triathlon. To make sure all Hurtlocker affiliates are moving in the same direction together, we suggest a balanced combination of programs, with a mix of both soft and hard activities. There are 8 activities, we suggest a mix cardio and combat activities to work on both fitness and technique. Each warmup focuses on a particular position, and gives attacking and defending options. Members are free to choose whichever activities they wish and can train for purely combat or fitness.
