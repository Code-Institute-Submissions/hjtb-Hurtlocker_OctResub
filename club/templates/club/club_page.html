Hurtlocker

About

The Hurtlocker Training System is a unique approach to martial arts and triathlon. To make sure all Hurtlocker affiliates are moving in the same direction together, we suggest a balanced combination of programs, with a mix of both soft and hard activities. There are 8 activities, we suggest a mix cardio and combat activities to work on both fitness and technique. If you're interested in joining please take a look at our membership options. Members are free to choose whichever activities they wish and can train for purely combat or fitness. Our instructors are the best in the business, they will build you up by putting you in the Hurtlocker.
